About the Project

An AI-powered calorie and nutrition estimator that uses deep learning to analyze food images and estimate their caloric and nutritional values. The system is a standalone web-based application that integrates AI models for food recognition and volume estimation. It will interact with a nutrient database to provide nutritional information. The application will be accessible via web browsers.
