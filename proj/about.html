Project Abstract

Our project is a practical and easy-to-use web application that helps users estimate the calorie and nutrient content of food just by uploading a picture. Using image recognition with a CNN model, the system identifies the food and compares it with a reliable database to provide nutritional details. It's built to support health-conscious individuals and fitness enthusiasts to track and manage their diet efficiently.
